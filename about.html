You are on the About Page

This page gives you a behind-the-scenes look at the purpose and goals of this website.

Hairstyle and More is a student project created to help users understand their hair type and receive personalized styling and haircut recommendations. Whether you have straight, wavy, curly, or coily hair, the site offers practical insights tailored to your routine and preferences.

This project was developed for the MSE 543 course at the University of Waterloo and demonstrates core principles of user-centered design, usability testing, and iterative development. All content is intended for educational purposes only.

Want to get started? Take the haircut quiz and discover your personalized style!

Have feedback or ideas for improvement? We'd love to hear from you! Reach out through the course discussion board or contact the developer directly.
